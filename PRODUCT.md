# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Professionals, developers, creatives, and students navigating high-cognitive-load workdays. They are looking for clean, non-jittery alternatives to caffeine and synthetics to manage stress and sustain attention.

## Product Purpose
Clario exists to offer functional, organic adaptogenic gummies that target specific mental states: Smooth Focus, Calm & Clarity, and Energy & Flow. The landing page acts as a premium digital experience that communicates organic science, premium flavor, and functional utility, driving direct-to-consumer subscriptions and sales.

## Brand Personality
Warm, organic, clinical yet sensorial, and highly refined. Clario balances technical efficacy with organic softness, avoiding the sterile coldness of typical medical supplements and the loud, gamified aesthetics of cheap candy brands.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS Templates**: Flat, grid-based layouts with blue accents and standard geometric sans-serif fonts.
- **Sterile Pharmaceutical Portals**: Harsh white backgrounds, sterile tables, and zero tactile or sensorial cues.
- **Acid-Maximalism / Loud Energy Brands**: Neon-on-black palettes, aggressive uppercase typography, and overstimulating animations.

## Design Principles
- **Sensory Resonance**: The UI should feel physical, textured, and alive, mirroring the organic form and flavor of the gummies.
- **Choreographed Focus**: Spacing, sizing, and transitions must guide the eye naturally through one primary concept at a time, preventing cognitive overwhelm.
- **Organic Deceleration**: Motion must feel weighted, smooth, and natural (using inertia and physics-based curves) rather than sudden, robotic, or elastic.
- **Trust Through Science**: Present clinical data and ingredients with clean, structured elegance, proving that wellness is backed by biochemistry.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Full WCAG 2.2 AA compliance.
- Clear focus indicators for keyboard navigation.
- Accessible contrast ratios for copy across all dynamic theme states (Sage Green, Lavender, and Gold).
